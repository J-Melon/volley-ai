## Positive framing

Describe what the thing IS and DOES, not what it lacks. The positive surfaces the actual work; a prohibition fences a hole and does not fire. Lead every section with what the system is, owns, or does. Before writing, scan for `not`, `never`, `no longer`, `instead of`, `doesn't`, `isn't`, `cannot`, `without`, and rewrite to positive shape.
