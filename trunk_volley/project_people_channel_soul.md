Soul is the medium the players channel, not an agent. People act; soul is what they wield. In docs and prose the players are the subject: they channel soul to levitate, gather soul by volleying, and spend channelled soul through items.

**Why:** 2026-05-27, the soul system doc. I wrote soul as the subject throughout ("soul lifts the player", "the rally runs on soul", "the climb soul drives"). Josh: "soul is not the actor, people are, people channel soul."

**How to apply:** rephrase with a person as the agent and soul as what they channel. Avoid "soul lifts the player", "soul causes both", "soul drives the rally". Pairs with [[project_arc_rises_with_levitation]] (reframe its "soul causes both" the same way) and [[feedback_item_fiction_is_not_design]].
