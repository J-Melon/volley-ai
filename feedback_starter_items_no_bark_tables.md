The legacy `designs/01-prototype/design/items.md` realises each item with a three-row Default / Power-revealed / Post-Break "Description" table of interior-thought barks. The **starter set does not use this.** Josh trimmed the table out of Cadence during SH-436, and on SH-438 (the realisation pass) rejected it again on the friendship bracelet: "we don't need the descriptions."

**Why:** the starter-items doc is deliberately terser than the legacy items.md. The realisation answers "what object" and "why interesting" through the object-fusion line and the effect. "How it develops" is the three mechanical upgrade levels (base/upgraded/max), which are gameplay work tracked separately (SH-439), not a flavour-text bark progression on the card.

**How to apply:** a realised starter-item card is the Cadence shape: name, role line, one fused "thing + twist" physical line, then the effect. Do not add the Default/Power/Post-Break table. Do not re-propose it. Related: [[project_items_are_gameplay_changers]], [[feedback_describe_before_naming]].
