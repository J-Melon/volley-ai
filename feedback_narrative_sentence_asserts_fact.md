**The rule.** Editor mode on narrative docs. Josh writes first drafts. I work the three editorial registers: developmental (is the structure right, are load-bearing claims present, is anything missing or doing double work, does the doc match its goal), line (voice, sentence rhythm, clarity, word choice, transitions, repetition), and copyedit (grammar, spelling, punctuation, style guide). Don't stop at copyedit; most of an editor's value is in the first two registers. Additions go through chat for agreement before they land. Every candidate passes the sense pass before delivery.

**Sense pass per sentence.** Name what the sentence asserts. If the honest answer is an atmosphere word like "warmth" or "closure" or "rhythm", cut. If the answer involves a banned shape, rewrite.

**Banned shapes.**

- Body-parts-as-knowers ("shoulders know", "the body remembers").
- Vague time-modifiers ("what X already knows", "what X has yet to learn").
- Closing-for-rhythm sentences whose function is the paragraph's landing.
- Personification of inanimates, subject form ("the garden offers", "fate brings") or possessive ("Construction's climb", "the garden's warmth"). Attribute to the animate party or rephrase.
- Enumeration-as-style: three- or four-item comma+and lists doing decorative work ("one venue, one coach, one mechanic, and a two-phase encounter"). Rewrite into prose that names the mechanism.
- Defining sentences that do not define. "A milestone happens at a venue" locates; "A milestone is one step in the climb" defines. Definition first; geography and structure in following sentences.

**Pre-write checks.** Hold a specific fact (no fact, no sentence). Pin every referent in the same sentence. Default to subject-verb-object.

**For minion briefs.** Preload this memory on narrative-writer dispatch; the discipline lives in the preload, not in repeated brief prose.

**Origin.** High-entropy prose passing surface check while asserting nothing ("wears what their shoulders already know how to carry"). Compressing context with these rules eliminates the failure mode. Editor-mode hardened on Volley outline rework SH-423.
