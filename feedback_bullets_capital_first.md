Every bullet item starts with a capital letter. "Main character" not "main character". Applies in Linear issue descriptions, ACs, source lists, and any other bulleted block in Volley written surfaces.

**Why:** Josh corrected SH-343 and SH-345 with "Capital letter first on all bullets" after I wrote "main character (SH-346)" / "first partner (SH-347)" lowercase, treating them as continuations of the lead-in sentence. Linear (and most prose conventions) treat each bullet as its own line item, capitalised.

**How to apply:** Before saving any bulleted list, scan for lowercase first words. Convert each to capital. Inline noun lists in flowing sentences stay lowercase; only bullet items get the capital.
