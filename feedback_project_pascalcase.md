Linear project names are Title Case and **two words maximum** per level. "Art Foundation", "Game Feel", "Codebase Organisation"; not "Security hygiene pass" (sentence case and three words).

**Why:** Projects sit in a list and need to read as proper nouns, not sentences, with every project scannable at a glance. Josh called this out on 2026-04-20 after "Security hygiene pass" and "First Partner Unlock" landed alongside correctly-shaped peers like "Art Foundation" and "Game Feel".

**How to apply:** When creating a Linear project, capitalise every significant word (skip articles, conjunctions, prepositions under 4 letters unless first) and keep each level to ≤2 words. Nested hierarchies follow the same rule per level, e.g. "Desktop Experience > Tech"; parent 2 words, child 1. If the concept needs more words, pick a tighter noun or split it into sibling projects.
