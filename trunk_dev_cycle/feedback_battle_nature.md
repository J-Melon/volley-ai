What a battle is, before how its verdict resolves. A battle is a confidence pass over a shipped
challenge, not an exhaustive audit ([[feedback_battle_is_a_confidence_pass]]); it is minion work,
dispatched by default rather than run in-thread ([[feedback_battle_is_minion_work]]); and it runs as
one standing loop end to end on every PR, including the one in front of me
([[feedback_battle_every_pr_autonomously]]).
