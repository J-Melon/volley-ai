Each Linear cycle for the Shuck team takes a name from a famous puppet, and the cycles march through the alphabet in order (cycle #1 was Animal at A). Which cycle is current and what letter is next is live state, not a fact to bake here: always check `cycles(orderBy: createdAt)` to see what has already shipped before proposing the next one.

Candidates at each letter (pick the one that best fits the tone of the cycle's work, warm register preferred):

- **A**: Animal, Alf, Abby Cadabby
- **B**: Bert, Big Bird, Beaker
- **C**: Cookie Monster, Crow T. Robot
- **D**: Dougal (The Magic Roundabout)
- **E**: Elmo, Ernie
- **F**: Fozzie Bear, Fraggle
- **G**: Gonzo, Grover
- **H**: Hacker T. Dog
- **J**: Judy (Punch & Judy)
- **K**: Kermit
- **L**: Lamb Chop
- **M**: Miss Piggy, Mr Punch
- **O**: Oscar, Orville
- **P**: Pinocchio, Parker (Thunderbirds)
- **R**: Rowlf, Roland Rat
- **S**: Sooty, Scooter, Statler
- **T**: Telly, Tinky Winky
- **W**: Waldorf
- **Y**: Yoda
- **Z**: Zoot, Zoe (Sesame Street)

**How to apply:** When asked to name or prep a cycle, pick the next letter in sequence relative to what has already shipped, then propose a specific puppet from that letter.
