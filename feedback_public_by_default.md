Volley is built in the open. Default destination for any new artefact (essays, research notes, design docs, process docs) is the public repo. When picking where a new file lands, lead with `designs/`, `ai/`, `README.md`-adjacent surfaces, etc.; public-facing paths.

**Why:** Josh confirmed 2026-04-26: "everything we do is public, remember this." This is the through-line of the open-development essay (`designs/research/the-case-for-open-development.md`). Treating things as private by default builds friction that compounds.

**How to apply:**
- When asked where to file something, default to a public path. Only suggest a private path (e.g. `designs/private/`, ignored scratchpads, memory) when there's a concrete reason: third-party copyrighted material (mood boards), pre-incorporation legal sensitivity, ephemeral working state, or content sync'd to Notion.
- When dispatching writing minions, brief them that the audience is public unless explicitly told otherwise. The voice is the studio voice (warm, generous, plain), not internal-tool voice.
- When a piece starts as scratchpad and grows good, default action is to promote it to `designs/research/` or similar, not to keep it scratch.
- The voice anchor is the open-development essay. Public surfaces inherit its register.
